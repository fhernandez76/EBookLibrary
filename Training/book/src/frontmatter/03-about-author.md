# About the Author

**Francisco Hernandez** is a Software Engineering Manager who has spent
his career building, scaling, and shipping web applications across the
.NET, JavaScript, and cloud ecosystems. He has led engineering teams from
the ground up, mentored engineers from junior to staff level, and is a
strong advocate of writing code that the next engineer on the team can
read without you in the room.

The EBook Library project began as an exercise in deliberately combining
a modern .NET 10 backend with two distinct frontend stacks (React and
Blazor) so that the same architecture could be examined from two angles.
This Book Edition is the long-form treatment of that experiment.

When not writing software or about it, the author can be found reading
fiction (in any of three languages) and trying not to add yet another
weekend project to the queue.

*Contact and follow-up corrections may be sent to the project repository's
issue tracker.*
